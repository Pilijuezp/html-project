My Favourite Sport

Football

I used to play in a team with my friends when I was living in Argentina, the team
name is La Brava. I really enjoy playing football, I like team sports. I think that
one of the best things about team sports is that they teach teamwork. In today's
world, working well with others is more important than ever. And while you can
learn teamwork in various settings, there's something special about learning it in
the context of a sport. Also I am a big fan of Argentina National team, and my
favourite players are:
